# Legal-Reasoning-and-Auditing-Engine
Stateful judicial reasoning engine for litigation strategy, precedent auditing, and factual alignment.

A stateful judicial reasoning and audit engine designed for litigation workflows. Rather than focusing on legal search, the system operates after retrieval—helping lawyers analyze precedent, align client facts with judicial reasoning, audit legal risks, and evaluate litigation strategies with transparent, evidence-backed outputs.

## Repository Contents

- 📄 Product Memo – Product vision, architecture, reasoning workflow, and design philosophy.
- 🎥 Technical Demo – Demonstration of the working prototype on real judgment packs. [Link Text](https://go.screenpal.com/watch/cO1jo4nurcq)

## Overview

The engine introduces several core capabilities:

- Deep factual alignment between client facts and judicial reasoning
- Precedent strength and doctrinal risk auditing
- Adaptive reasoning based on each judgment's legal lens
- Stateful case workspaces across multiple interactions
- Intent-aware defense strategy generation
